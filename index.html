About

I am james, and I am a Designer. I live in Ottawa, Ontario and conduct freelance work within the city. I first gained an interest in design during high school where I started to design logos for bands and posters for concerts. Since then I have expanded into graphic and web design. I have the ability to create beautiful user exeriences and flawless designs of all shapes and sizes.


I moved to Ottawa from Oxford, England when I was two years old. I grew up in a small town south of Ottawa called Manotick. Since then I have moved into the city to be closer to work and school. Some of the hobbies that I enjoy in my spare time would include: reading, painting, hockey, mountain biking, and attending concerts.
